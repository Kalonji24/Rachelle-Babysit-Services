About Me

I'm Not an Agency — I'm Just Someone Who Loves Kids

My name is Kalonji and I've been babysitting for over 6 years. It started with looking after my siblings and cousins, then neighbours started asking, and word just spread from there. I've never worked for a company — everything I know comes from real hands-on experience.

I'm reliable, patient, and I really care about the children I look after. I treat every child like they're my own. Parents — moms, dads, aunties, uncles, grandparents — all come to me when they need someone they can actually trust.

I'm not fancy, but I show up on time, I communicate clearly, and your kids will be happy, fed, and safe while you're away. That's my promise.

👶 Experienced with newborns, toddlers & school-age kids
🩹 Basic First Aid knowledge
🛡️ Police clearance available on request
📲 Regular photo & update messages to parents
🏠 In-home care at your place
